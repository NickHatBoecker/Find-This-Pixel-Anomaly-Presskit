== When will this game launch? ==
Find This Pixel Anomaly will launch 8th April 2026.

== What platforms will this game release on? ==
Find This Pixel Anomaly will be available on Steam only.
It is fully playable on Steam Deck.

== How much will it cost? ==
The full version with four maps and three difficulties will cost 4,99$.

== What will be available at the launch of the full version, apart from the demo contents? ==
Four maps in total (Museum, Mall, Hospital and Prison) and a new difficulty: Hard mode!

== How much playtime is to expected? ==
A successfuly run of one map takes about 18 minutes.

Let's assume you complete every map in a single run, in three difficulties, you would come up with approximately 4 hours of gameplay. After this you can start achievement hunting.
